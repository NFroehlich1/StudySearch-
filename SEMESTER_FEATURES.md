### 1. **Edit Semester Name**
- Click the "Edit" button on any semester card
- Change the semester name (e.g., "WS 2023", "SS 2026", "Past Courses")
- Perfect for tracking completed courses or planning future semesters

### 2. **Custom ECTS Goals**
- Set custom ECTS goals per semester
- Default is 30 ECTS, but can be adjusted for part-time study, thesis semesters, etc.
- Progress bars and calculations use your custom goal

### 3. **Add New Semesters**
- Click "Add Semester" button at the top of the planner
- Create unlimited semesters for:
  - Past/Completed courses
  - Current semesters
  - Future planning
  - Thesis/Internship semesters
- Each semester gets its own color and ECTS goal

### 4. **Delete Semesters**
- Click the "Delete" button to remove a semester
- Must keep at least 1 semester
- All courses remain in your list (they're just removed from that semester)

### 5. **Semester Colors**
- Customize semester colors to match your organization system
- Color applies to the semester indicator and progress bar
- Helps visually distinguish between different semesters
